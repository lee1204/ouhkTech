Institutional Review from Hong Kong Council for Accreditation of Academic and Vocational Qualifications
9 Jul 2020

Today, the representatives from Hong Kong Council for Accreditation of Academic and Vocational Qualifications (HKCAAVQ) review the courses offered by the Computing Programmes Team to ensure the quality of our programme (Bachelor of Computing with Honours in Internet Technology). Several graduates and undergraduate students of computing programmes were also invited to attend the meeting at Council Chamber at OUHK (Homantin Main campus). Thanks to graduates and students for taking the time to attend and providing comments about our programme to the reviewers.

 
The First VR Game about Blood Donation in Hong Kong - Cooperate with Hong Kong Red Cross
2 Jun 2020

Final Year Project Team LetsGoParty, consist of three students, Yhin Yip Hin CHOI, Sunny Kin San LAW and Ben Tsz Hong WONG, supervised by Professor Andrew LUI, cooperated with Hong Kong Red Cross to design the first-ever VR Game about Blood Donation in Hong Kong for their final year project titled “Educate positive image for teenagers about blood donation with Virtual Reality”.

Dr.Lee, Director of Hong Kong Red Cross, and Chelly Chu, Nurse of Hong Kong Red Cross were invited to evaluate our VR game on the demonstration day. They are satisfied with the VR game. They are willing to use this VR game to bring a positive image to break the teenagers’ fear of blood drawing and strengthen their interest in blood donation to support the Red Cross blood drawing service in the future.

Presentation at the International Conference EcoSta2019
25 Jun 2019

Cheng Ting Hin, Li Ka Lok, Wong Chun Lam, Lau Suet Ying and Ng Chak Long presented their final year project work 'Identification of pairs trading opportunities using copula-based conditional probabilities and machine learning models' in the International Conference on Econometrics and Statistics (EcoSta2019) in Taiwan on 25-27 June 2019. Apart from presenting their work, they also attended several researchers' presentations. It is a memorable event for all the team members.
