Study Tour to Finland 2019: Embracing Innovation in High-tech Economy
20-28 Oct 2019

Location: Helsinki and Rovaniemi, Finland

This study tour was organized by the Electronics and Computer Engineering Team and supported by the IEEE OUHK Student Branch. The study tour was sponsored by the Subsidy Scheme for International Exchanges (OUIE) and Reaching Out Award (ROA). Objectives were to provide students of OUHK with the opportunity to: (i) Gain a better understanding of the current technological developments in Finland; (ii) Exchange ideas with people who are in the academic and industrial sectors in Finland; and (iii) Appreciate how innovation benefits the society and contributes to building a high-tech and sustainable economy. The group of 24 students and staff visited universities, companies, and various sites including Aalto University, University of Helsinki, Nokia, Fazer, Arbonaut, National Library of Finland, Suomenlinna, Temppeliaukion Church, Helsinki Central Library, Smart Cities (Kalasatama and Jätkäsaari), Shaman Huskies Farm, Santa Claus Village, Ranua Zoo, and Arktikum Science Museum.

Study Tour to Guangzhou 2019: opportunities in the Greater Bay Area
12-13 Mar 2019

Study Tour to Guangzhou 2019 was organized by the Electronic & Computer Engineering Team and supported by the IEEE OUHK Student Branch. The tour was sponsored by the Hong Kong Chiu Chow Chamber of Commerce Ltd. – Winnie Ko Student Exchange Fund. Objective of the study tour was to provide students with the opportunity to: (i) gain a better understanding of the current technological developments in Greater Bay Area; (ii) exchange ideas with people who are in the academic and industrial sectors in Greater Bay Area; and (iii) appreciate how technology benefits the society. The group visited various places, including Huaqiangbei (華強北), Seg Plaza (賽格廣場), SegMaker Space (赛格眾創空間), 順德大盤魚, 南沙漁村, 福林農莊, numerous laboratories in the Guangdong University of Technology (廣東工業大學), and Guangdong Science Centre.
