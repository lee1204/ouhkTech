Technical Visit to Ngong Ping Sewage Treatment Works
1 Feb 2019

Students from engineering had a technical visit to the Ngong Ping Sewage Treatment Work (NPSTW) on 1 February 2019. Led by the staff of NPSTW, the group visited the reclaimed water pond, as well the operating facilities for screening and degritting, secondary (biological) treatment, tertiary treatment and disinfection. They were also shown the process flow between the equipment. NPSTW came into operation in late 2005 as the first tertiary treatment plant in Hong Kong. It makes use of advanced chemical, biological, filtering and disinfection processes. The reclaimed water is purified and odourless, and is safe for various non-potable uses.

 
Technical Visit to Hong Kong Science Park
27 Nov 2018

A group of 40 full-time students from electronic and computer engineering and I.T. visited the Hong Kong Science Park on 27 November 2018. Led by HKSTP staff, the group had a tour to the Data Studio, which is a convenient collaboration environment for co-creating data applications between the business sector and developer community. Then talks were given by Mr. Barry Leung, Founder and Director of Lambda Sense Limited, and Mr. Kelvin Wan, founder and Managing Director of BCONDUIT. In the talks, the students were introduced to various AI and big data applications in various sectors, as well as insights in starting companies in the Science Park.
