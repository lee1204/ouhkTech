Visit to EMSD Education Path
28 Nov 2019

A group of 17 electronic and computer engineering students and staff visited the Electrical and Mechanical Services Department (EMSD) Education Path on 28 November 2019. In the guided tour, EMSD staff introduced their organization and responsibility, as well as various innovative green building technologies used in the EMSD HQ. These included 3 exhibition areas and over 2000 rooftop photovoltaic panels.

Company Visit to Green Tomato Limited
31 Oct 2019

A group of computing students visited Green Tomato Limited in Kwun Tong. Green Tomato is an award winning company with a number of famous products such as Hong Kong Movie and Timable. The staff introduced the facilities and workplace of the company. They also shared the latest developments in the IT industry and tips on how to enter the industry, and answered students' questions. Students learnt more details about the operations and management of project workflow, which is very helpful to the students' career planning.

If you want to know more details about the company, please visit: http://www.greentomato.net.

 
Technical Visit to Government Flying Service
24 May 2019

Location: Government Flying Service Headquarters

A group of 25 electronic and computer engineering students and staff visited the headquarters of Government Flying Service (GFS) on 24 May 2019. In the guided tour, GFS staff introduced their work domain and responsibility, such as search and rescue, air ambulance, and internal security. The staff also led the group to various areas inside the headquarters including the air command and control centre and hangar.
