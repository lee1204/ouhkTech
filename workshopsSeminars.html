2019 International Conference on Open and Innovative Education (ICOIE 2019)
10-12 Jul 2019

The Open University of Hong Kong has successfully held The 2019 International Conference on Open and Innovative Education (ICOIE 2019), was held on 10–12 July 2019 at Jockey Club Campus. The aim of the Conference is to provide an international platform for academic exchanges and sharing of quality research relevant to open and innovative education.

Apart from academicians, researchers, professionals and practitioners from different countries, the representatives of the computing programme team, including professors, teachers, and final year students also gave presentations of their papers at the Conference. In addition, our year-one students Lok-Tung Cheng, Rui-Feng Yu and Min-Hong Chan had the honor of presenting the papers entitled “An On-line Intelligent Application for Learning Japanese Language”.

 
Presentation at the International Conference EcoSta2019
25 Jun 2019

Cheng Ting Hin, Li Ka Lok, Wong Chun Lam, Lau Suet Ying and Ng Chak Long presented their final year project work 'Identification of pairs trading opportunities using copula-based conditional probabilities and machine learning models' in the International Conference on Econometrics and Statistics (EcoSta2019) in Taiwan on 25-27 June 2019. Apart from presenting their work, they also attended several researchers' presentations. It is a memorable event for all the team members.

The 2019 alumni sharing activity was held at 25 May 2019 (Sat).
